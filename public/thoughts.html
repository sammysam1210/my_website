November 20, 2017 - Thankfulness
There's always plenty to be thankful for, but especially during this time of thanksgiving, I'm reminded more and more to express my thankfulness to people. November 15, 2017 - Rejection & Self Pity
I know that I don't have much experience in working in industry, and that's why I want to get more experience by working. It's hard to get those chances though, because they look at your past experience, and even though Jessie recommended me, I wasn't able to get the internship. I definitely need to mature though, as there were 15 people that applied to a single position, making it unlikely for me to get it. When I got rejected, I felt a lot of self-pity, whining to myself, and essentially feeling sad for myself. However, this doesn't do anything, and that's something that I need to recognize. What instead I need to do, if I am unable to get internships and experience, is to gain more experience on my own, experience in both CS and life. For CS, it'll be through creating personal projects where I sharpen my skills and add to my knowledge, while experience in life will be reflection, helping me to mature and grow up more.
